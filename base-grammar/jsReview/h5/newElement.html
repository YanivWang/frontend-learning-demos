谁 ( sheí ) : who, whom

谁( sheí )  is a question word used for people. Asking a question in Chinese is easy: simply place the question word in part of the sentence you are asking about. Also don’t forget to add the relevant question punctuation at the end of the sentence.

Chinese Translation

她(tā)是(shì)谁(sheí)？

Who is she?

谁(sheí)想(xiǎng)喝(hē)水(shuǐ)？

Who wants to drink water?

你(nǐ)找(zhǎo)谁(sheí)？

Who are you looking for?
